About Us

Transforming education through community empowerment

Our Vision

To create a future where every child has access to quality education, and every graduate has a dignified livelihood through teaching.

Our Mission

We aim to identify unemployed graduates, train them to become competent private tutors, connect them with local students and schools, and uplift education at the grassroots through community-driven empowerment.

Meet the Founder

Naheed N. Hussain

Mr. Naheed N. Hussain Is a Passionate Trainer, Education-Social Entrepreneur, Educator with Over 25 plus Years of Experience in The Field of Education. He Holds Four Master's Degrees, Including One in Law, and Has Successfully Conducted More Than 100 Training Programmes and Seminars for Teachers and Students Across Various Platforms.

Driven By His Commitment to Educational Empowerment and Social Impact, He Has Launched the Transformative Project "Shikshasathi"—A Visionary Initiative to Identify and Mentor Unemployed Graduates and Postgraduates Aspiring to Become Successful Teachers. Through This Platform, He Aims to Train Them as Effective, Result-Oriented Tutors and Facilitate Their Employment as Private Tutors as Well as In Premier Private Schools, Thereby Bridging the Gap Between Passion, Skill, And Opportunity in The Teaching Profession.

How It Works

Your journey to becoming a successful tutor

1

Registration & Verification

Free enrollment for aspiring tutors with data collection and documentation.

2

Training & Certification

Online & offline training by expert mentors on pedagogy, communication, child psychology, and subject expertise.

3

Tutor Matching

Students matched with tutors in their localities with personalized attention and demo classes.

4

School Placement

High-performing tutors recommended for jobs in reputed private and CBSE schools.

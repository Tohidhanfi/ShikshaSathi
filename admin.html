About Us

Transforming Education Through Community Empowerment

Our Vision

To Create A Future Where Every Child Has Access To Quality Education, And Every Graduate Has A Dignified Livelihood Through Teaching.

Our Mission

We are committed to discovering skilled yet unemployed graduates and postgraduates, providing them with quality training to become confident and effective educators.

Meet the Founder

Naheed N. Hussain

Mr. Naheed N. Hussain Is a Curious Learner,Passionate Trainer and Educator with Over 25 plus Years of Experience in The Field of Education. He Holds Four Masters' Degrees, Including One in Law, and Has Successfully Conducted More Than 100 Training Programmes and Seminars for Teachers and Students Across Various Platforms.

Driven By His Commitment to Educational Empowerment and Social Impact, He Has Launched the Transformative Project "Shikshasathi"A Visionary Initiative to Identify and Mentor Unemployed Graduates and Postgraduates Aspiring to Become Successful Teachers.

Naheed N. Hussain

Founder & CEO

Challenges & Our Solutions

Transforming Problems into Opportunities

Lack of Teaching Experience

New graduates often lack practical teaching skills and classroom confidence

Practical Training + Mentorship

Comprehensive training programs with expert mentorship and hands-on experience

Trust Issues with Parents

Parents need assurance about tutor credibility and teaching quality

Verified ID Cards + Demo Classes

Identity verification and demonstration sessions to build confidence

Quality Assurance

Ensuring consistent teaching standards across all tutors

Parent Feedback + Periodic Reviews

Regular assessments and parent satisfaction surveys for continuous improvement

Distance Barriers

Geographic limitations affecting accessibility and convenience

Hyperlocal Tutor-Student Matching

Smart matching system for local connections and convenience

Sustainability

Long-term program viability and growth sustainability

CSR partnerships + NGO tie-ups

Strategic partnerships for sustainable impact and growth
